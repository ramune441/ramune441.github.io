How to Use Lovent

Get started in minutes. No complex setup required.

1 📍

Set your search areas

Tap the area selector in the top bar and enter a neighborhood, city, or region. You can save up to 5 areas. Try "Downtown Manhattan", "Shibuya", or your neighborhood.

2 📋

Browse the event list

Events for the next 14 days appear automatically. Scroll through the card list or switch to map view with the map icon in the top bar.

3 🔍

Filter events

Tap the filter chip at the top to open the filter panel. Select categories (Music, Food, Sports, etc.), date range (Today / Tomorrow / This Weekend), or enable Free Only to narrow results.

4 🗺️

Switch to Map view

Tap the map icon to see all events plotted on Google Maps. Tap a marker to see a preview card. Tap the card to open directions.

5 📅

Tap ♥ to save to Google Calendar

Tap the heart icon on any event card or detail page. The event is instantly added to your Google Calendar with a reminder. You can customize the reminder time (day before, day of, 1 hour before) in the event detail.
